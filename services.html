Our Services

A diverse portfolio of continuous remote monitoring services providing advanced health solutions for patients and additional revenue to practices.

Cardiac Monitoring

Remote observation of cardiac activity through Holter, MCOT, HRV processes as well as Event types of monitoring. 24 hours up to 30 days of live remote cardiac monitoring recording every second of the patient's heart activity.

Learn More

Physiological Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of patients' vitals at the convenience of their homes under the remote supervision of certified medical professionals. Monitor ECG, blood pressure, SPO2, weight, and vitals for home-based chronic care management.

Learn More

Medicine Dispensary

Smart medication management solutions designed to help patients remember to take medication on time and every day, especially for those living with conditions like Alzheimer's disease or dementia.
