What we offer

Mobile Cardiac Outpatient
Telemetry (MCOT)

48 hours up to 30 days a real-time, outpatient cardiac monitoring system that is automatically activated and requires no patient intervention to transmit an arrhythmia when it occurs.

Live Event
Monitor

Recording of the heart's electrical activity during symptoms using a portable device. It records the same information as an electrocardiogram (ECG), but for longer duration of time.

Live Holter
Monitor

A portable device that continuously records the rhythm of the heart, typically for 24-48 hours
